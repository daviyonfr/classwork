phase vocab

concept phase

The concept phase encourages and helps you take a mindful approach to your project before you formally start it.The concept phase, also known as project initiation, is the first stage in Agile software development. It involves the product owner, business analysts, the development team and future users, who establish a vision of the project, and define its scope,objectives, and goals.
